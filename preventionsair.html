ROLE OF
PREVENTION:

Air pollution is a significant environmental issue that can have detrimental effects on human health, ecosystems, and the overall quality of life. Preventing and addressing air pollution requires a comprehensive approach involving individuals, communities, industries, and governments

WAYS TO PREVENT

Transition to Clean Energy Sources:


1.Promote and invest in renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydropower


2.Encourage the use of electric vehicles and public transportation

Strict Emission Standards:


1.Implement and enforce stringent emission standards for industries and vehicles


2.Regularly update and monitor compliance with emission regulations

Promote Sustainable Practices:


1.Encourage sustainable agricultural practices to reduce the use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides


2.Implement and support sustainable forestry practices

Reduce Deforestation:


1.Enforce laws against illegal logging and promote reforestation programs


2.Protect and preserve natural habitats to maintain biodiversity

Waste Management:


1.mplement effective waste management strategies to reduce open burning of waste


2.Promote recycling and proper disposal of hazardous materials

Promote Energy Efficiency:


1.Encourage energy-efficient technologies in industries, homes, and transportation


2.Implement building codes that prioritize energy efficiency
